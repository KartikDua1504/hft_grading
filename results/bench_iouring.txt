--- Analysis ---
Both engines achieve ~500K TPS, bottlenecked by the single-threaded
dummy_exchange server (not the client transport). The io_uring engine's
advantage manifests under higher server-side concurrency where:
  1. Fixed files eliminate fdget/fdput overhead per SQE
  2. SQPOLL eliminates io_uring_enter() syscalls
  3. Linked send+recv chains reduce submission round-trips
  4. Registered buffers eliminate copy_from_user per send

True io_uring advantage requires a server capable of saturating
the client, or a real exchange with actual matching latency.
